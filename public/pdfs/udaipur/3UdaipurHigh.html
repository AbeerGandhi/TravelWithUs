3-Day Trip Itinerary - Udaipur

City: Udaipur

Description: A beautiful garden with fountains, kiosks, and a lotus pond, built for the royal ladies of Udaipur.

Duration: 90 minutes

Lake Pichola Boat Ride

Description: A scenic boat ride on Lake Pichola, offering views of Udaipur's palaces and ghats.

Duration: 120 minutes

Dinner at a Rooftop Restaurant

Description: Enjoy a meal with stunning views of the lake, showcasing traditional Rajasthani art and crafts.
