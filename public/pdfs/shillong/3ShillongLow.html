Description: A serene park with scenic views, perfect for picnics and leisurely walks.

Duration: 60 minutes

Description: Shillong Peak offers panoramic views of the city and surrounding hills.

Duration: 120 minutes

Don Bosco Museum

City: Shillong

Description: A museum showcasing the life and work of Don Bosco, a Catholic priest who dedicated his life to helping underprivileged youth.

Duration: 60 minutes

Umiam Lake

City: Shillong

Description: A serene lake surrounded by lush hills, offering stunning views and opportunities for boating and fishing.

Mawsmai Cave

Description: A limestone cave with stunning stalactites and stalagmites, offering a glimpse into the geological wonders of Meghalaya.

Ward's Lake

City: Shillong

Description: A picturesque artificial lake in Shillong, offering scenic views and boating opportunities.

Duration: 60 minutes

State Museum

City: Shillong

Description: The State Museum showcases Meghalaya's rich history, culture, and tribal heritage.

Duration: 60 minutes
