3-Day Trip Itinerary - Jaipur

Day 1 - 22 Oct, 2024

Hawa Mahal

City: Jaipur

Description: The Hawa Mahal, or Palace of Winds, is a unique architectural marvel built in 1799, known for its honeycomb-like facade and intricate windows.

Duration: 120 minutes

City Palace

City: Jaipur

Description: The City Palace is a grand palace complex built in the 18th century, showcasing the rich history and culture of Jaipur.

Duration: 120 minutes

Jantar Mantar

City: Jaipur

Description: An 18th-century astronomical observatory with intricate instruments for tracking celestial bodies.

Duration: 60 minutes

Albert Hall Museum

City: Jaipur

Description: A beautiful museum showcasing a diverse collection of art, artifacts, and sculptures.

Duration: 60 minutes

Chokhi Dhani

City: Jaipur

Description: A cultural village experience showcasing Rajasthani traditions, crafts, and entertainment.

Duration: 120 minutes

Amer Fort

Jal Mahal

City: Jaipur

Description: A palace built in the middle of Man Sagar Lake, Jal Mahal is a stunning architectural marvel.

Nahargarh Fort

City: Jaipur

Description: A majestic fort perched on a hill overlooking Jaipur, offering panoramic views and a glimpse into the city's rich history.

Duration: 120 minutes

Jaipur Bazaar

City: Jaipur

Description: A vibrant marketplace in Jaipur, offering a kaleidoscope of Rajasthani crafts, textiles, and jewelry.

Duration: 120 minutes

The Forresta

City: Jaipur

Description: A luxurious hotel in Jaipur, known for its elegant rooms and rooftop dining.

Duration: 120 minutes

Galtaji Temple

Birla Mandir

Jaipur Heritage Walk

City: Jaipur

Description: A vibrant walking tour showcasing Jaipur's rich history, architecture, and local markets.

Duration: 120 minutes

Laxmi Narayan Temple

City: Jaipur

Description: A beautiful temple dedicated to Laxmi and Narayan, known for its intricate architecture and peaceful atmosphere.
