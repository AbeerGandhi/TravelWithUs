2-Day Trip Itinerary - Jaipur

Day 1 - 22 Oct, 2024

Hawa Mahal

City: Jaipur

Description: The Hawa Mahal, or Palace of Winds, is a unique architectural marvel built in 1799, known for its honeycomb-like facade and intricate windows.

Duration: 120 minutes

City Palace

City: Jaipur

Description: The City Palace is a grand palace complex built in the 18th century, showcasing the rich history and culture of Jaipur.

Duration: 120 minutes

Jantar Mantar

City: Jaipur

Description: An 18th-century astronomical observatory with intricate instruments for tracking celestial bodies.

Duration: 60 minutes

Amber Fort

City: Jaipur

Description: A majestic fort perched on a hill, offering stunning views and glimpses into the rich history of Jaipur.

Duration: 180 minutes

Chokhi Dhani

City: Jaipur

Description: A cultural village experience showcasing Rajasthani traditions, crafts, and entertainment.

Duration: 120 minutes

Day 2 - 23 Oct, 2024

Jal Mahal

City: Jaipur

Description: A palace built in the middle of Man Sagar Lake, Jal Mahal is a stunning architectural marvel.

Duration: 60 minutes

Nahargarh Fort

City: Jaipur

Description: A majestic fort perched on a hill overlooking Jaipur, offering panoramic views and a glimpse into the city's rich history.

Duration: 120 minutes

Albert Hall Museum

City: Jaipur

Description: A beautiful museum showcasing a diverse collection of art, artifacts, and sculptures.

Duration: 90 minutes

Govind Devji Temple

City: Jaipur

Description: A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, known for its intricate architecture and vibrant festivals.

Duration: 60 minutes

Bapu Bazaar

City: Jaipur

Description: A bustling market in Jaipur, known for its traditional Rajasthani handicrafts, textiles, and jewelry.

Duration: 120 minutes

Laxmi Mishthan Bhandar

City: Jaipur

Description: A popular sweet shop in Jaipur, known for its delicious and affordable sweets.

Duration: 60 minutes
