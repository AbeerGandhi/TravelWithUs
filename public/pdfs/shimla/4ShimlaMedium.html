3-Day Trip Itinerary - Shimla

Day 1 - 23 Oct, 2024

Jakhoo Hill

City: Shimla

Description: Jakhoo Hill is a popular tourist destination in Shimla, known for its Hanuman Temple and panoramic views.

Duration: 120 minutes

The Ridge

City: Shimla

Description: The Ridge is a pedestrianized promenade in Shimla, offering stunning views of the Himalayas.

Duration: 60 minutes

Christ Church

City: Shimla

Description: A historic church built in 1836, offering stunning views of the Himalayas.

Duration: 60 minutes

Mall Road

City: Shimla

Description: Mall Road is a bustling pedestrian street offering scenic views of the lake and hills.

Duration: 120 minutes

Scandale Point

City: Shimla

Description: A scenic viewpoint offering panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Duration: 60 minutes

Indian Institute of Advanced Study

City: Shimla

Description: A former Viceroy's residence, now a renowned research institute.

Duration: 60 minutes

Duration: 180 minutes

Himalayan Nature Park

City: Shimla

Description: A scenic park showcasing Himalayan flora and fauna, with hiking trails and panoramic views.

Duration: 120 minutes

Fagu

City: Shimla

Description: A picturesque village in the Himalayas, Fagu offers stunning views and hiking trails.

Duration: 120 minutes

Chadwick Falls

City: Shimla

Description: A scenic waterfall near Shimla, offering refreshing views.

Duration: 60 minutes

Day 3 - 25 Oct, 2024

Viceregal Lodge

City: Shimla

Description: The Viceregal Lodge, now a museum, was the summer residence of the British Viceroy of India.

Duration: 120 minutes

Tara Devi Temple

City: Shimla

Description: A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Tara Devi, offering panoramic views of Shimla.

Duration: 60 minutes

Summer Hill

City: Shimla

Description: Known for its scenic views and colonial architecture.

Duration: 120 minutes

The Gaiety Theatre

City: Shimla

Description: A historic theatre known for Victorian architecture and performances.

Duration: 60 minutes

Shimla State Museum

City: Shimla

Description: Showcases the history and culture of Himachal Pradesh with artifacts from the region's past.

Duration: 60 minutes
