3 Days Trip in Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu, India

Day 1

Location City Description Duration (minutes)
Kanyakumari Beach Kanyakumari Relax at the iconic beach where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean meet, known for its stunning sunrise and sunset views. 120
Our Lady of Ransom Church Kanyakumari Visit this peaceful church by the sea, known for its beautiful Gothic architecture. 45
Gandhi Memorial Kanyakumari A free-to-visit memorial dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi, with unique architecture inspired by Hindu temples. 30
Vattakottai Fort Kanyakumari Explore this coastal fort with scenic views of the sea and surrounding hills. Ideal for a relaxing picnic. 60
Local Handicraft Market Kanyakumari Browse local shops for budget-friendly souvenirs, seashell art, and handicrafts unique to the region. 90

Day 2

Location City Description Duration (minutes)
Sunset View Point Kanyakumari A popular, free viewpoint offering spectacular sunset vistas over the ocean. 60
Thanumalayan Temple Suchindram A beautiful temple known for its musical pillars and intricate carvings, with minimal entry fees. 90
Tsunami Monument Kanyakumari A moving memorial dedicated to the lives lost during the 2004 tsunami. 30
View of Thiruvalluvar Statue Kanyakumari Enjoy a free view of the Thiruvalluvar Statue from the mainland or beach, symbolizing Tamil heritage. 30
Kanyakumari Lighthouse Kanyakumari Climb to the top of the lighthouse for a nominal fee to enjoy panoramic views of the city and coastline. 45

Day 3

Location City Description Duration (minutes)
Sothavilai Beach Kanyakumari Relax at this peaceful, less crowded beach, ideal for a quiet morning stroll. 120
Mathur Aqueduct Arumanai Walk along one of the tallest aqueducts in South Asia, surrounded by scenic landscapes. 60
Chitharal Jain Monuments Chitharal Explore ancient Jain rock-cut monuments and sculptures dating back centuries, with minimal entry fees. 90
Thanumalayan Temple Suchindram Visit this historic temple once again to experience the local culture, musical pillars, and peaceful ambiance. 90
Evening Stroll and Souvenir Shopping Kanyakumari Spend your final evening in Kanyakumari strolling around the local markets, picking up souvenirs and snacks. 60
