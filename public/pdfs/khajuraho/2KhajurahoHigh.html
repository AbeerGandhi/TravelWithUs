2-Day Trip Itinerary - Khajuraho

Day 1

Western Group of Temples

City: Khajuraho

Description: Known for intricate carvings, the Western Group of Temples is the main attraction in Khajuraho.

Duration: 180 minutes

Eastern Group of Temples

City: Khajuraho

Description: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Eastern Group of Temples showcases intricate carvings and sculptures depicting Hindu mythology.

Duration: 180 minutes

Khajuraho Archaeological Museum

City: Khajuraho

Description: Houses artifacts from the Khajuraho temples, offering insights into their history and craftsmanship.

Duration: 60 minutes

Khajuraho Sound and Light Show

City: Khajuraho

Description: A captivating multimedia show illuminating the intricate carvings of the Khajuraho temples.

Duration: 60 minutes

Day 2

Jain Temples

City: Khajuraho

Description: A beautiful Jain temple known for its intricate carvings and peaceful atmosphere.

Duration: 120 minutes

Raneh Falls

City: Khajuraho

Description: Raneh Falls is a scenic waterfall located in the Vindhya Range, known for its natural beauty and surrounding forests.

Duration: 120 minutes

Panna National Park

City: Khajuraho

Description: Panna National Park is a wildlife sanctuary known for its diverse flora and fauna, including tigers, leopards, and sloth bears.

Duration: 180 minutes

Khajuraho Market

City: Khajuraho

Description: Known for its ancient Hindu and Jain temples, the market is ideal for souvenirs and local crafts.

Duration: 120 minutes
