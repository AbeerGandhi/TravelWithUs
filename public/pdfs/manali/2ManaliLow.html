2-Day Trip Itinerary - Manali

Day 1 - 23 Oct, 2024

Hidimba Devi Temple

City: Manali

Description: A unique temple dedicated to Hidimba Devi, wife of Bhima from the Mahabharata, known for its distinctive wooden structure.

Duration: 120 minutes

Vashisht Hot Springs

City: Manali

Description: Natural hot springs with therapeutic benefits, offering a relaxing soak amidst scenic Himalayan views.

Duration: 120 minutes

Mall Road

City: Manali

Description: A bustling pedestrian street offering great views of Manali’s scenic surroundings and a variety of shopping and dining options.

Duration: 120 minutes

Tibetan Monastery

City: Manali

Description: A serene Buddhist monastery offering peaceful surroundings and stunning Himalayan views.

Duration: 120 minutes

The Lazy Dog

City: Manali

Description: A popular spot for its cozy atmosphere and great food with Himalayan views.

Duration: 120 minutes

Day 2 - 24 Oct, 2024

Solang Valley

City: Manali

Description: A picturesque valley known for its stunning landscapes and adventure activities like skiing, paragliding, and trekking.

Duration: 240 minutes

Rohtang Pass

City: Manali

Description: A high mountain pass in the Pir Panjal Range, offering breathtaking views of snow-covered peaks.

Duration: 180 minutes

Manu Temple

City: Manali

Description: An ancient temple dedicated to Manu, the first man in Hindu mythology, with stunning views of the surrounding hills.

Duration: 60 minutes

Old Manali

City: Manali

Description: A charming village known for its laid-back vibe, quaint cafes, and stunning views of the Himalayas.

Duration: 120 minutes

Johnson's Cafe

City: Manali

Description: A popular cafe known for its delicious food and cozy atmosphere with mountain views.

Duration: 120 minutes
